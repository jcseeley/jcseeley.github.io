About Me

Jase Seeley is an aspiring software developer currently attending Epicodus coding bootcamp. He resides in the Woodstock neighborhood of Portland, OR with his girlfriend, Katie. Jase has been an advocate for a plant-based lifestyle since beginning his own vegan journey in 2017. He is an avid NBA and Formula1 fan, movie buff, gamer, and craft beer enthusiast.

Background

Jase was born and raised in Concord, NH. While there, he spent 8 years working for the Grappone Automotive Group. Starting in the detail center, he quickly worked his way up to become a key member of the used inventory team. He attended Manchester Community College's Automotive Technology program. After tiring of the auto industry and his home state, he knew it was time for a change. At the beginning of 2019, he packed his car and headed for the west coast. Upon landing in Oregon, Jase pursued a job in the bustling Portland beer industry. He quickly found work as a draft technician at Northwest Draft. During his time there, he attended the Micro-Matic dispense institute in CA where he learned how to install a variety of different draft systems. After being laid off due to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, Jase relied on gig work to get by until making the decision to enroll at Epicodus.
